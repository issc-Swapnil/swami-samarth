Biography

Shree Swami Samarth Maharaj of Akkalkot was a divine incarnation and spiritual master who guided thousands of devotees on the path of devotion, truth, and righteousness in the 19th century. His miraculous deeds and profound teachings continue to inspire devotees across generations.

About Swami Samarth

Shree Swami Samarth Maharaj, also known as Akkalkot Swami, is believed to be an incarnation of Lord Dattatreya. He lived in Akkalkot during the 19th century and was revered for his divine wisdom, miracles, and teachings. Devotees from across India seek his blessings and follow his path of Bhakti (devotion).

Teachings

  • Have unwavering faith in God.
  • Serve others selflessly.
  • Practice truth and discipline.
  • Chant the divine name regularly.
  • Chant the name of God continuously.
  • Remain devoted to your Guru.
  • Perform your duties with sincerity.
  • Stay away from ego, greed, and pride.
  • Help those in need and live a simple life.

Miracles

  • Healed the sick and cured incurable diseases.
  • Blessed devotees with children and peace of mind.
  • Read minds and revealed past lives.
  • Appeared in dreams and guided devotees even after samadhi.
